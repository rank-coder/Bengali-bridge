### Translate to Bangla (Use all possible honorific levels for both subjects and objects) (Optionally, use Bengali script)
1. How are you? 
   apni këmon ach*en*. (high honor)
   tumi këmon ach*o*. (normal)
   tui këmon ach*ish*. (low honor)
2. He is your (tomar/apnar) friend (bondhu).
   *she* tomar bondhu.
   *tini* tomar bondhu.
   *tini* *apnar* bondhu.
3. How are you all?
   *tomra* këmon ach*o*. (normal)
   *apnara* këmon ach*en*. (high honor)
   *tora* këmon ach*ish*. (low honor)
4. I won't eat mango/mangoes.
   *ami* am kha*bo* na.
5. We live (thaka) in Kolkata.
   *amra* kolkata thaki. (✔️) *amra* kolkatay thaki. (✔️) *amra* kolkatate thaki. (✔️)
6. I am Bangladeshi.
   *ami* baŋladeshi.
   আমি বাংলাদেশি। 
7. Is your son (chele) here (ekhane)?
   ekhane ki *tomar* chele ache? (✔️)
   *apnar* chele ekhane? (✔️)
   *apnar* chele ki ekhane? (✔️)
   *tomar* chele ki ekhane ache?
   *tomar* chele ekhane ache ki?
   *tor* chele ki ekhane ache?
8. Do you know (jana/para) Bengali?
   *apni* ki baŋla janen?
   *tumi* baŋla jano?
   *tui* ki baŋla jan*ish*?
9. Where (kothay/koi) do you live?
   *apni* kothay thak*en*?
   *tui* kothay thak*ish*?
   *tumi* kothay thak*o*?
   *apni* koi thaken?
   *tui* koi thakish?
   *tumi* koi thako?
10. Are you Bengali/Bangalee?
    *tumi* ki baŋali?
    *tui* ki baŋali?
    *apni* ki baŋali?
    *apni* baŋali ki?
11. Do you speak (bölo) English? (*trick question*)
    para = *to be able to*
    *tumi* ki iŋreji paro?
    *tumi* ki iŋreji bolte paro? (Are you able to speak English?)
    *tumi* ki iŋreji jano? (Do you know English?)
    ❌ tumi ki iŋreji bölo? ❌
### Translate to English and Optionally Write out in Bengali Script
1. apni kothay thaken. [[Where do you live?]]
2. ami ëkṭa köla khabo.[[I will eat a banana.]]
3. ami shöb (all) likhechi. [[I have written it all.]]
4. amra khub bhalo achi. [[We are doing very well.]]
5. era kara? [[Who are these people? (normal honor)]]


ke = who?
ke ke = who? (pl.)
kara = who? (pl.)
