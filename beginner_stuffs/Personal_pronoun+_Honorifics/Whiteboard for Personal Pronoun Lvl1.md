Whiteboard for [[Personal Pronoun]] Level 1
# Subject Pronouns
## 1st person singular subject
1. ami onupöm. [I am Onupom.]
2. ami jani. [I know.]
 > S + O + V (Default Sentence Structure)
3. ami bhat khai. [I eat rice.]
## 1st Person Plural Subject
 ami + ra = *amra*
1. amra chattro. [We are students.]
2. amra jani. [We know]
3. amra bhat khai. [We eat rice.]
## 2nd Person Singular Subject
*tumi*
1. tumi rana. [You are Rana.]
2. tumi jano. [You know.]
3. tumi bhat khao. [You eat rice.]
## 2nd person plural subject
tumi + ra = *tomra*
1. tomra chatro. [You are students.]
   We don't need plural nouns.
2. tomra jano. [You all know.]
3. tomra bhat khao. [You all eat rice.]
## 3rd person singular subject
*she* [He/She]
1. she Rana. [He is Rana.]
2. she jane. [He/She knows.]
3. she bhat khay. [He/She eats rice.]
## 3rd person plural subject
she + ra = *tara* [They]
1. tara chatro. [They are students.]
2. tara jane. [They know.]
3. tara bhat khay. [They eat rice.]

# Posssessive Determiner/Pronouns
## 1st Person Singular Possessive
ami + r = *amar* [My/Mine]
1. amar boi [My book]
2. boiṭi amar. [The book is mine.]
## 1st Person Plural Possessive
ami + der = *amader* [Our/Ours]
1. amader skul. [Our school]
2. baṛiṭi amader. [The house is ours.]
## 2nd person singular possessive
tumi + r = *tomar* [Your/Yours]
1. tomar boi. [Your book]
2. boiṭi tomar. [The book is yours.]
## 2nd person plural possessive
tumi + der = *tomader* [Your/Yours]
1. tomader skul [Your school]
2. baṛiṭi amader. [The house is yours.]
## 3rd person singular possessive
she + r = *tar*
1. tar boi [His/Her book]
2. boiṭi tar. [The book is his/hers.]
## 3rd person plural possessive
she + der = *tader*
1. tader desh [Their country]
2. gaṛiṭi tader. [The car is theirs.]

# Objects
> objects end in /ke/ [Me]
## 1st Person Singular Object
ami + ke = *amake* , amay
1. she amake cene. [She knows me.]
## 1st person Plural Object
ami + der + ke = *amaderke* [Us]
1. she amaderke cene. [She knows us.]
## 2nd Person Singular Object
tumi + ke = *tomake* , tomay
1. ami tomake cini na. [I don't know you.]
## 2nd Person Plural Object
tumi + der + ke = *tomaderke*
1. amra tomaderke cini na. [We don't know you all.]
## 3rd Person Singular Object
she + ke = *take* [Him/Her]
1. ami take cini. [I know him.]
## 3rd Person Plural Object
she + der + ke = *taderke* [Them]
1. amra taderke cini na. [We don't know them.]
